Welcome to the Offical Website for the
Mercedes-Benz M100-Powered Automobiles

The M-100s are among the rarest, most powerful and luxurious automobiles ever produced by Mercedes-Benz.

These vehicles represent the state-of-the-art in engineering by combining safety, comfort, handling and speed without compromise. During their 18-year reign as Mercedes-Benz's flagship sedans, less than 17,000 were produced. Here we pay tribute to these important motorcars that once prowled the world's highways without peer. Their original ownership roster reads like a who's-who among dignitaries, celebrities and motorsports professionals - but today they are a rare sight. M-100 owners and aficionados please feel free to take advantage of any of the resources found here.
